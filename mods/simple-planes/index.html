General

Upgrades

General

To add fuel just right click with coal while sitting in the plane.
Crimson and warped planes are immune to fire.

Small Planes

They can hold one player.

Large Planes

They can hold two entities at once.

Recipes

Special Fans

Redstone Fan

Has range equal to given redstone power.

Configurable Fan

You can change it's range by right clicking on it. Crouch + right click sets it back to zero. Max range equals 15 blocks.

Sticky Fan

Works like gold fan, but doesn't push entities which are touching it.

Miscellaneous

Sticky Boots

Fans don't push you while you are standing on ground.

Propeller

A crafting component.
