Basic Fans

Basic fans just always push entities. Better mineral makes fan faster with longer range.

Special Fans

Redstone Fan

Has range equal to given redstone power.

Configurable Fan

You can change it's range by right clicking on it. Crouch + right click sets it back to zero. Max range equals 15 blocks.

Sticky Fan

Works like gold fan, but doesn't push entities which are touching it.

Miscellaneous

Sticky Boots

Fans don't push you while you are standing on ground.
